Here are some Interesting Facts about Nepal:

  • Only country in the world to not have a rectangular flag

  • Home to Mt Everest and 8 of the 10 tallest mountains in the world!

  • The birthplace of Buddha

  • Never been colonized by another nation

  • A monarchy until 2008

  • The year is 2077 in Nepal. New years is celebrated on April 13th.

Here are some Tips for first time visitors:

  • Don't drink water from street vendors as it may be indigestible for non residnets

  • Make sure you have a local guide, some locals will try to rip you off for being foreign

  • Traffic rules may not always be followed so don’t solely trust traffic signs while travelling

  • Don't travel without the required medicine and vaccinations

  • Having a tour guide will drastically make your stay more pleasant

  • People of Nepal are friendly, however don’t forget to ask people for permission before snapping pictures of them.

  • The outlets in Nepal are Type C,D and M outlet rather than the Type A used in North America
